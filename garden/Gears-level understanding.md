Gears-level understanding is a level of understanding where one models the things inside a thing in study, not just its behavior, so they can do experiments about its internals, ensure that the behavior of the internals make system's behavior generalize OOD.

Think of an opaque box of gears, if you know how it works inside and how the components (gears) interact it will be obvious why the exposed gear is acting accordingly and properly disagree with anyone claiming it may act otherwise whoever they are

A heuristic for testing whether some understanding I have is gears-level is to ask myself: "Does it feel like \[subject of matter\] is running on magic and could just... violate my understanding anytime?"
