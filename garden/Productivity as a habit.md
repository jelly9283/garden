[[Productivity|Productivity]] can be turned into a habit, by doing productivity consistently, usually with a trigger. I should use writing repeatedly as a trigger, and most importantly, not do unproductive stuff while I am writing.

It takes a long time to form a productivity habit, as with any other habit. It takes about 30 days to fully form the habit.

[[Digital intentionality|Digital intentionality]] becomes easier if productivity is a habit.
