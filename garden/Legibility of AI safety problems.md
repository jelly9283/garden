[[AI safety|AI safety]] problems are...
- **legible** to policymakers when they are unlikely to allow deployment of an AI while these problems remain open
- **illegible** when policymakers don't realize their importance, usually because it is hard to think about/grasp.

This means that working on highly legible AI problems could accelerate AI timelines, which is bad. This also means that making illegible problems highly legible is as good as solving them.
