Understanding how [[Natural Abstractions|natural abstractions]] emerge can help us understand...
- what's in an AI's mind
- how it computes things
- whether it thinks like a human or not
- how we can manipulate abstractions inside its mind
- how we can reverse-engineer the AI into human-understandable parts
- etc.

A concrete objective here is to build a [[Thermometer for abstractions|thermometer for abstractions]]. Of course we can have other goals for this agenda as well but maybe this is the main one.
