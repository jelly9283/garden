An abstraction of a thing X is a short summary of X which removes unneccessary details of it and leaves only useful information about X.

example: A mouse being chased by a cat doesn't need to know each and every individual position and orientation of the cat's individual atoms and particles, it only needs to know the general speed and direction at which it moves to decide how to escape

This is relevant to [[Natural Abstractions|the natural abstractions agenda]].
